Hello.

I’m Ham or "Hamilton". I am a UC architect, engineer and leader. I have spent the majority of my career leading product, strategy and operational teams in the UCC/AV realm and have had the privilege to learn and grow in some of the world’s premiere environments in the Unified Communications industry.

I pride myself on creating extremely accessible physical environments predicated on finding the intersection between cost and user productivity. I’ve won awards for my builds and been noted in several publications. With an eye to the future, I’m excited about leveraging the continuing ubiquity of IOT peripherals and the potential of centralization of VC delivery (utilizing virtual machines) to create highly modular and low cost deployments.

I find myself fortunate to have had highly influential and disruptive mentors like Medallia CIO Ashwin Ballal and Lumibuild Founder Carlos Martinez (formerly Uber, Tesla, Zynga, BlackRock) who groomed me to forecast, think big picture and constantly take good bets. Standing on their shoulders, I have successfully created higher functioning teams through prioritizing remote support, creating cross-functional IT workflows and challenging teams to think as owners. Due to these philosophies, I have run extremely high functioning teams with both great cost of scale and world-class user experience scores (eNPS).
